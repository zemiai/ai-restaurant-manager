# The AI Restaurant Manager Kit for Raspberry Pi 5

## Product Overview

**The AI Restaurant Manager Kit** is a complete, self-hosted automation solution designed specifically for independent restaurant owners who want to harness the power of AI without expensive monthly SaaS subscriptions.

Built on the rock-solid foundation of **Raspberry Pi 5** and **OpenClaw/Clawdbot**, this kit transforms a $80 single-board computer into a 24/7 intelligent restaurant assistant that handles POS integration, vendor bill automation, voice announcements, inventory management, and daily operations briefings.

## Problem We Solve

Independent restaurants face three critical challenges:

1. **Tool Fragmentation** — Separate systems for POS, inventory, scheduling, voice announcements, and vendor management create data silos and manual work.

2. **Subscription Overload** — SaaS platforms charge $50-$500/month per location, adding thousands in annual costs while owning your data.

3. **Operational Chaos** — Without automation, owners and managers spend hours on manual tasks: checking emails for invoices, updating menus, creating checklists, and scrambling for daily briefings.

---

## Our Solution

The AI Restaurant Manager Kit consolidates all restaurant operations into one cohesive, automated system:

- **Toast POS Integration** — Real-time sales tracking, menu sync, labor insights
- **Gmail Automation** — Auto-categorize and process vendor bills and invoices
- **MiniMax TTS Announcements** — Voice announcements for kitchen calls, daily specials, staff notifications
- **Daily Briefings** — AI-generated morning reports covering sales, inventory, staff, and priorities
- **FOH Checklists** — Digital opening/closing checklists with completion tracking
- **Menu Management** — Centralized menu updates that sync across all systems
- **Compounding System** — Automated upsells and promotional sequencing
- **Security Framework** — Local-first data storage with backup and monitoring

## Why Raspberry Pi 5?

- **Affordability** — Core system cost: ~$80-120
- **Reliability** — Runs 24/7 with minimal power consumption (<10W)
- **Privacy** — All data stays local; no cloud dependency
- **Expandability** — Add HATs, cameras, displays as needed
- **Community** — Massive support ecosystem
